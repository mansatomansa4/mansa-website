## For Mentees

### Getting Started

**1. Create Your Account**
- Visit the MentorHub homepage
- Click "Sign Up" in the top navigation
- Complete the registration form
- Verify your email address

**2. Browse Available Mentors**
- Navigate to "Community" → "Mentorship"
- Use the search bar to find mentors by name, expertise, or company
- Filter by expertise category using the dropdown menu
- View mentor profiles to learn about their background

### Booking a Mentorship Session

**Step 1: Choose a Mentor**
- Click on a mentor card to view their full profile
- Review their:
  - Bio and experience
  - Areas of expertise
  - Session rating
  - Total completed sessions
  - Availability calendar

**Step 2: Select Time Slot**
- View the mentor's available time slots
- Click on your preferred date and time
- Slots are shown in your local timezone

**Step 3: Provide Session Details**
- Enter the topic you'd like to discuss
- Add a brief description of what you hope to achieve
- Explain any specific questions or areas of focus
- Click "Request Booking"

**Step 4: Wait for Confirmation**
- The mentor will review your request
- You'll receive a notification when they respond
- Once confirmed, you'll get the meeting link

### Managing Your Bookings

**View All Bookings:**
- Go to "Community" → "Mentorship" → "My Bookings"
- See all your upcoming and past sessions

**Filter Bookings:**
- **All:** View all bookings
- **Pending:** Awaiting mentor confirmation
- **Confirmed:** Approved sessions with meeting links
- **Completed:** Past sessions (ready for feedback)

**Cancel a Booking:**
- Click "Cancel" on any pending or confirmed booking
- Confirm cancellation in the popup
- The mentor will be notified automatically

**Join a Session:**
- Click "Join Meeting" on confirmed bookings
- Opens the video call link provided by your mentor
- Arrive 2-3 minutes early for technical checks

### Leaving Feedback

After completing a session:

1. Navigate to "My Bookings"
2. Find the completed session
3. Click "Leave Feedback"
4. Rate your experience (1-5 stars)
5. Write detailed feedback about:
   - What you learned
   - How the mentor helped
   - Areas for improvement
6. Click "Submit Feedback"

**Why Feedback Matters:**
- Helps mentors improve
- Guides other mentees in choosing mentors
- Builds trust in the community

### Best Practices for Mentees

**Before the Session:**
- ✓ Prepare specific questions
- ✓ Research your mentor's background
- ✓ Test your video/audio setup
- ✓ Be ready 5 minutes early
- ✓ Have a notebook ready

**During the Session:**
- ✓ Be punctual and respectful
- ✓ Listen actively
- ✓ Ask clarifying questions
- ✓ Take notes
- ✓ Be open to feedback

**After the Session:**
- ✓ Review your notes
- ✓ Implement advice given
- ✓ Leave honest feedback
- ✓ Follow up if needed
- ✓ Apply what you learned

## For Mentors

### Becoming a Mentor

**Application Process:**

1. Navigate to "Community" → "Mentorship" → "Become a Mentor"

2. **Step 1: Basic Information**
   - Write your professional bio (50-500 characters)
   - Select your expertise areas (minimum 1)
   - Choose your timezone
   - Add company and job title
   - Specify years of experience

3. **Step 2: Profile Photo**
   - Upload a professional headshot
   - Maximum file size: 5MB
   - Accepted formats: JPG, PNG
   - Recommended: 400x400px minimum

4. **Step 3: Social Links** (Optional)
   - Add your LinkedIn profile
   - GitHub profile (for technical mentors)
   - Twitter/X account
   - Portfolio website

5. **Step 4: Review & Submit**
   - Review all information
   - Agree to mentor guidelines
   - Submit application
   - Wait for admin approval (typically 24-48 hours)

### Mentor Dashboard

Access your dashboard at "Community" → "Mentorship" → "Mentor Dashboard"

**Dashboard Sections:**

**1. Statistics Cards**
- Total sessions conducted
- Completed sessions
- Total mentees helped
- Average rating

**2. Quick Actions**
- View all sessions
- Manage availability
- Edit profile
- Toggle accepting requests

**3. Upcoming Sessions**
- Next 5 sessions displayed
- Mentee information
- Session topics
- Quick actions (confirm, add link, cancel)

**4. Performance Metrics**
- Response rate
- Completion rate
- Average session duration

### Managing Your Profile

**Edit Profile:**
1. Click "Edit Profile" from the dashboard
2. Switch between Preview and Edit modes
3. Update any of the following:
   - Bio
   - Profile photo
   - Expertise areas
   - Social links
   - Accepting requests toggle
4. Click "Save Changes"

**Profile Visibility:**
- Approved mentors appear in mentor browse page
- Your profile shows:
  - Professional information
  - Expertise areas
  - Rating and session count
  - Availability status

### Setting Your Availability

**Two Types of Availability:**

**1. Recurring Weekly Slots**
- Set regular weekly time blocks
- Example: Every Monday 2:00 PM - 3:00 PM
- Automatically applies to future weeks

**Adding Recurring Slots:**
1. Go to "Availability Management"
2. Select "Recurring Schedule" tab
3. Choose day of week
4. Select start time
5. Select end time
6. Click "Add Time Slot"
7. Repeat for all regular availability

**2. Specific Dates**
- One-time availability
- Example: January 15th, 10:00 AM - 11:00 AM
- Useful for special sessions or exceptions

**Adding Specific Dates:**
1. Select "Specific Dates" tab
2. Choose the date
3. Select start time
4. Select end time
5. Click "Add Time Slot"

**Removing Slots:**
- Click the trash icon next to any slot
- Confirm deletion
- Slot removed immediately

**Best Practices:**
- Keep at least 10 slots available
- Update availability weekly
- Block out personal time
- Allow buffer time between sessions

### Managing Session Bookings

**Booking Workflow:**

**1. New Booking Request**
- Receive notification of new request
- Review mentee's topic and description
- Check your calendar

**2. Confirm or Decline**
- Click "Confirm Booking" to accept
- Click "Decline" if unavailable
- Confirmed bookings move to "Confirmed" tab

**3. Add Meeting Link**
- Click "Add Meeting Link"
- Paste video call URL (Zoom, Google Meet, etc.)
- Save the link
- Mentee can now join

**4. Conduct the Session**
- Be punctual and prepared
- Review the session topic beforehand
- Have resources ready to share
- Take notes if needed

**5. Complete Session**
- Mark as complete after finishing
- Optionally add notes for your records
- Wait for mentee feedback

**Booking Actions:**

**Confirm:**
- Accept the booking request
- Sends confirmation to mentee
- Adds to your schedule

**Add Meeting Link:**
- Required before session
- Can be Zoom, Google Meet, Teams, etc.
- Mentee receives immediate access

**Cancel:**
- Only if absolutely necessary
- Provide cancellation reason
- Mentee receives notification

### Mentor Guidelines

**Professional Conduct:**
- ✓ Be respectful and inclusive
- ✓ Maintain confidentiality
- ✓ Provide constructive feedback
- ✓ Be honest about your expertise
- ✓ Stay within scheduled time

**Communication:**
- ✓ Respond to bookings within 24 hours
- ✓ Confirm meetings 24 hours in advance
- ✓ Provide clear meeting links
- ✓ Notify mentees of any changes
- ✓ Follow up after sessions

**Quality Standards:**
- ✓ Prepare for each session
- ✓ Tailor advice to individual needs
- ✓ Share relevant resources
- ✓ Set realistic expectations
- ✓ Track mentee progress

**What to Avoid:**
- ✗ Promoting products or services
- ✗ Guaranteeing specific outcomes
- ✗ Sharing confidential information
- ✗ Cancelling sessions without reason
- ✗ Being unprepared or distracted

### Tips for Effective Mentorship

**1. Active Listening**
- Let mentees speak first
- Ask clarifying questions
- Paraphrase to confirm understanding
- Identify underlying concerns

**2. Goal-Oriented Approach**
- Define clear session objectives
- Break down complex goals
- Provide actionable steps
- Set follow-up milestones

**3. Share Real Experiences**
- Use personal anecdotes
- Discuss challenges you faced
- Explain your decision-making
- Be honest about failures

**4. Resource Sharing**
- Recommend books or courses
- Share useful tools
- Connect to relevant people
- Provide templates or frameworks

**5. Follow-Up**
- Summarize key takeaways
- Send resources mentioned
- Check progress in future sessions
- Celebrate mentee achievements

## For Administrators

### Admin Panel Access

**Accessing Admin Panel:**
1. Login with admin credentials
2. Navigate to `/admin/mentorship`
3. Use the fixed sidebar for navigation

**Admin Roles:**
- **Admin:** Full access to mentorship platform
- **Superadmin:** Full system access including user management

### Admin Dashboard Overview

**Statistics Section:**
- Total mentors (approved + pending)
- Pending mentor applications
- Total bookings
- Platform average rating

**Recent Activity Feed:**
Shows the last 10 activities:
- New mentor applications
- Completed sessions

- Feedback submissions

**Quick Actions:**
- Review pending mentors

- Manage users
- View analytics

**System Health:**
- API status
- Response times

### Managing Mentor Applications

**Review Process:**

1. Navigate to "Mentors" in admin sidebar
2. Use filter tabs:
   - **All:** All mentors
   - **Pending:** Awaiting approval
   - **Approved:** Active mentors
   - **Inactive:** Deactivated accounts

3. Review application:
   - Click "View Details" on any mentor
   - Review their:
     - Professional background
     - Expertise areas
     - Social profiles
     - Bio quality

4. Make decision:
   - **Approve:** Activates mentor profile
   - **Reject:** Declines application with reason

**Approval Criteria:**
- ✓ Complete and professional bio
- ✓ Relevant expertise
- ✓ Professional photo
- ✓ Verifiable background
- ✓ Clear communication

**Rejection Reasons:**
- Incomplete profile
- Inappropriate content
- Lack of relevant experience
- Duplicate application
- Terms violation

### Managing Bookings

**Booking Oversight:**

1. Navigate to "Bookings" in admin sidebar
2. Filter by status:
   - **All:** All bookings
   - **Pending:** Awaiting confirmation
   - **Confirmed:** Approved sessions
   - **Completed:** Past sessions
   - **Cancelled:** Cancelled bookings

3. View booking details:
   - Mentor and mentee information
   - Session topic and description
   - Date, time, and duration
   - Status and meeting link
   - Feedback and rating (if completed)

**Admin Actions:**

**View Details:**
- See complete booking information
- Access mentor/mentee profiles
- Review session notes

**Export Data:**
- Click "Export to CSV"
- Downloads all bookings matching current filter
- Includes: dates, participants, status, ratings

**Monitor Quality:**
- Review session feedback
- Identify issues or conflicts
- Track mentor performance
- Ensure policy compliance

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General

**Q: Is MentorHub free to use?**
A: Yes, all mentorship sessions are currently free.

**Q: How long are typical sessions?**
A: Most sessions are 30-60 minutes.

**Q: Can I book multiple sessions with the same mentor?**
A: Yes, you can book as many sessions as needed.

### For Mentees

**Q: How far in advance should I book?**
A: Book at least 2-3 days in advance for best availability.

**Q: Can I reschedule a booking?**
A: Cancel the current booking and create a new one.

**Q: What if my mentor doesn't show up?**
A: Contact support immediately at support@mentorhub.com.

### For Mentors

**Q: How long does approval take?**
A: Typically 24-48 hours.

**Q: Can I set different rates for different sessions?**
A: Currently, all sessions are provided free of charge.

**Q: Can I mentor multiple expertise areas?**
A: Yes, select all relevant expertise areas in your profile.

### For Admins

**Q: How do I add new expertise categories?**
A: Contact the technical team or add via Django admin.

**Q: Can I bulk-approve mentors?**
A: Not currently supported; review each application individually.

**Q: How do I export platform data?**
A: Use the CSV export feature in the bookings section.

## Glossary

**Mentee:** A user seeking guidance and advice
**Mentor:** An experienced professional providing guidance
**Booking:** A scheduled mentorship session
**Availability Slot:** A time period when a mentor is available
**Session:** A one-on-one mentorship meeting
**Feedback:** Post-session rating and review
**Expertise:** Areas of professional knowledge and skill
